Bike Project

This project is a series of tests in which I record and analyze accelerometer data obtained by mounting my iPhone to my bike and performing various actions.

Here is a list of all of the documented tests and experiments:

Experiment 1 Click here to download the raw data for experiment 1

During this experiment, I rode my bmx bike on asphalt, sidewalk, and down my backyard and recorded the accelerometer values. For the analysis, I focused on observing the amount of bumpiness in each of the three trials.

Experiment 2 Click here to download the raw data for experiment 2

During this experiment, I recorded the accelerometer values while bunnyhopping and going over a small plastic ramp. For the analysis, I focused on detecting jumps and smoothing data.
